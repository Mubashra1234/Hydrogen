The World’s Cheapest
Green Hydrogen

NewHydrogen is developing a disruptive technology that uses clean
energy and water to produce the world’s cheapest green hydrogen.

Hydrogen is the cleanest and most abundant element in the universe, and we can’t live without it. Hydrogen is the key ingredient in making fertilizers needed to grow food for the world. It is also used for transportation, refining oil and making steel, glass, pharmaceuticals and more.

Hydrogen is a zero-emission fuel that produces only water vapor when used. However, hydrogen does not exist in its pure form on Earth and must be extracted from sources that contain hydrogen,

Working with research teams at UCLA and UC Santa Barbara, NewHydrogen is helping to advance the green hydrogen revolution.

Our goal is to help usher in the green hydrogen economy that Goldman Sachs estimated to have a future market value of $12 trillion.
